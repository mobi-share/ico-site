What is Mobi?

Mobi is a carpooling mobile application available on both iOS and Android that connects drivers traveling to a certain destination with riders who are also traveling a similar way. The app includes integrated payment portals, a chat platform and peer-to-peer reviews to ensure a quality service for both the rider and also the driver. This allows drivers to offset their costs for the commute and provides a discounted fare for the riders.

Pick a route

Simply enter your starting location and your destination. Then enter your desired pick up time and select the days you want to comute. That's right, Mobi helps plan your whole week!

Choose a commuter

Choose the person you want to travel with! Mobi allows you to select the person you want to commute with by offering everyone who is heading in that direction.

Chat before you ride

You can chat with your fellow commuter before the ride if you need to let them know something or just want to get to know them!

What is Mobi Token?

And what benefit does it provide?

The Mobi Token is an ERC20 token that will integrate into our rideshare application, providing a number of important features.



Reduced Fees

Removing the need for 3rd party payment gateways means that transaction fees will be much more affordable for everday commuters!

Trustless Payments and Dispute Mediation

Smart contracts will remove the need for 3rd party confirmation. Dispute mediation will allow appropriate outcomes for disputes between parties.



Cross Platform Incentives

Blockchain technology will allow users paying with Mobi coin a 0% service fee using the app. There is also a welcome reward, referral reward and transaction reward system which are explained in detail in our whitepaper.

Transparency of Information

Removing the need for a central party elminates the risk of data manipulation. Mobi will leverage blockchain technology to operate a rating and review system for drivers which cannot be tampered with and is therefore 100% trust worthy.
